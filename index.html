Why this project?

I wanted to make a webpage that was able to showcase what I was comfortable with while allowing me to challenge myself with new aspects of coding that I am unfamiliar with. I decided to focus on keyframe animations. Keyframe animations were something I thought beyond my scope. I have always considered myself a back-end coder. Web design is something that requires a level of creativity that I am struggling to discover. I wanted this portfolio project to be something of an experiment. Something to flex my abilities and show that I can be a well-rounded coder.

Pros:

I was surprised at how well I was able to make use of keyframe animations. I made use of a random number generator to determine the position of the stars in the background.

Cons:

The sizing and position of the sun has been giving me issues. The biggest problem I had with this project was having so much freedom to do whatever I wanted but not having the knowledge to know how to make the ideas come to life.
