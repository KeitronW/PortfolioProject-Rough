about this project

This project was not a streamlined process. I wanted to make a webpage that was able to showcase what I was comfortable with while allowing me to challenge myself with new aspects of coding that I am unfamiliar with. I decided to focus on keyframe animations. Keyframe animations were something I thought beyond my scope. I have always considered myself a back-end coder. Web design is something that requires a level of creativity that I am struggling to discover. I wanted this portfolio project to be something of an experiment. Something to flex my abilities and show that I can be a well-rounded coder.
